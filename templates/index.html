CITIZEN COMPLAINT PROCESSING SYSTEM

Welcome to the online complaint processing portal

Admin Login User Login

The citizen complaint processing system is an one step online portal for lodging complaints online.
This online portal makes the process of lodging complaints easy and quick.
People don't need to go to the respective departments to file a complaint instead they can do it by sitting at home.
New users have to register themselves by providing their details and login to the portal using their username and password.
